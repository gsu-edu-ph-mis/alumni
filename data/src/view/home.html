It is an online platform designed to help the university manage and connect with its graduates.

You need a computer or mobile device, an internet connection, a web brower (E.g. Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, etc.), and a username and password issued by the Alumni Affairs Office.

On the login page, look for "Forgot your password? Reset here," then click the link. Once redirected to the password reset page, enter a valid email address associated to your alumni account and click Reset. You will receive an email with instructions to verify your request. If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder or contact the Alumni Affairs Office for assistance.
